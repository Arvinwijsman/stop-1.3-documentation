Element ogc:PropertyIsBetween

Namespace http://www.opengis.net/ogc
Definitie

The PropertyIsBetween element is defined as a compact way of encoding a range check. The lower and upper boundary values are inclusive.
Informatie

PropertyIsBetween is alleen toegestaan bij se:SemanticTypeIdentifier geo:kwantitatieveNormwaarde.

Informatiemodel ProperttIsBetween (eigenschap van Operator (kwantitatief))
